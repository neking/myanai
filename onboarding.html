👥 Create Staff Accounts

Set up your team for POS operations

Each staff member (cashier, manager) needs a PIN for POS login. Here's how:

  • Cashier PIN: A simple 4-digit code (e.g., 1234)
  • Manager PIN: Used for refunds, discounts, reports
  • Role Assignment: Cashier, Manager, or Admin
  • Permissions: Control what each role can do

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add more branches?
Go to Settings → Branches. Your plan limits the number of branches. Upgrade to add more.
Can I use MyanAi on my phone?
Yes! The POS works on phones and tablets. We recommend an iPad or large Android tablet for best experience.
What payment methods are supported?
Currently: Cash and Card (with card reader). Mobile payment integration coming soon.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted, backed up daily, and complies with data protection standards.
